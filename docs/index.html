About Holos

Holos is a whole-farm model and software tool that estimates greenhouse gas (GHG) emissions based on information entered for individual farms. The model was developed by Agriculture and Agri-Food Canada to explore agricultural management practices that may reduce GHG emissions from Canadian farms.

Disclaimer

The results produced by Holos are estimates only and are not intended to replace professional judgment. The emission factors and methodologies used in Holos are based on the Intergovernmental Panel on Climate Change (IPCC) guidelines and Canadian-specific research. These factors are subject to scientific uncertainty and may be updated as new research becomes available.
